Inspiration + credit:

This CPU design is based off the design from this excellent youtube video by youtuber: FULXOR

"FULXOR" made his CPU in digital logic sim out of pure logic gates. I saw that and thought "hey could I make that in logisim ?" and here we are. It's not a complete carbon copy there are numerous adjustements and optimisations that have been added but I just thought I'd say the cpu system I have made is inspired/based upon the one showed in "FULXOR's" video.

https://youtu.be/6u2rzB3HtXM?si=fro5X8VywcSyZOoa

Opcodes:

I can have a total of 16 instructions. At the moment I have only made 9

0001 – Display

0010 – Sub

0011 – Add

0100 – instr_reg_str

0101 – Acc_str

0110 – PC_Clr

0111 – Acc_Clr

1000 – DatReg_Cr

1001 – OutReg_Clr
